Who am I?

I'm Yusuf Özmavi, a Full Stack Mobile Developer with experience working at a development agency and as an indie maker. I specialize in creating beautiful, functional, and user-centered mobile applications that solve real-world problems.

With a passion for clean code and intuitive design, I've developed multiple successful apps that are available on the App Store. I've implemented payment systems using RevenueCat and have experience with webhooks and Google Cloud services. I enjoy the entire development process from conceptualization to deployment and maintenance.

My technical expertise includes both native (Swift, SwiftUI) and cross-platform (Flutter, React Native) development, allowing me to choose the right technology for each project's unique requirements. I pride myself on delivering apps that not only look great but also perform exceptionally well.

When I'm not coding, I'm constantly learning new technologies and exploring ways to improve user experiences in mobile applications.

My Development Process

How I approach mobile app development projects

1. Discovery

I begin by understanding your business goals, target audience, and project requirements. This phase involves market research and defining the app's core functionality.

2. Design

Creating wireframes and prototypes to visualize the user interface and experience. We'll iterate on designs until they perfectly align with your vision.

3. Development

Building the application with clean, maintainable code using the most appropriate technology stack for your project needs, whether it's Swift, Flutter, or React Native.

4. Testing

Rigorous testing across multiple devices and scenarios to ensure your app is bug-free, performs well, and provides an excellent user experience.

5. Deployment

Handling the app store submission process and ensuring your app meets all guidelines for a successful launch.

6. Maintenance

Providing ongoing support, updates, and improvements to keep your app running smoothly and adapting to user feedback.
