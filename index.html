— About

A little background.

I'm 25, based in Antalya, and I've been shipping iOS apps full-time for three years. I'm a university student studying something completely unrelated to computer science — everything I know about Swift, Flutter, and subscriptions I taught myself on the way to shipping the next thing.

I focus on subscription apps because they're the only mobile business model that actually compounds. One good app with retention can quietly pay rent for years. I've watched it happen — twenty times now. Most of them found their audience through ASO, not paid ads. Cheaper, slower, more honest.

Working with me is direct and async. We talk on Telegram or email, you get the build in 2–4 weeks, and I stay around for a month after launch to fix what real users break. No standups, no project managers, no decks.
